Voter ID Laws

In most cases you do not need to show an ID.

If you are asked to show an ID please show one the following forms:

  • A copy of a current and valid photo ID OR
  • A copy of a current utility bill, bank statement, or paycheck OR
  • A government document that shows your name and address.
